Calabar

Calabar festival

Celebrated in the Nothern Nigeria

Dubbed Africas largest carnival, Calabar Carnival is a must-see event for visitors and tourists. The carnival began in 2004 as a means of attracting tourists to the city of Calabar with a month-long event featuring displays of African culture through music, dance, drama, and fashion, all in an explosion of bright colors.
